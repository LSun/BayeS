Introduction

Comparison of Lasso and elastic net (both glmnet), Bayesian bridge (BayesBridge), spike-and-slab priors (BoomSpikeSlab), and SBR on the diabetes data set.

library(lars)
library(glmnet)
library(BoomSpikeSlab)
library(BayesBridge)
source("../code/SBRr.R")
data("diabetes")
X = diabetes$x
y = diabetes$y
y = y - mean(y)
X2 = diabetes$x2
